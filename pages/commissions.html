Workflow

Payment > First Version > Revisions > Final Delivery

• All my commissions are delivered “as soon as possible” depending on workload.
• I take feedback throughout the project to have the product correctly at the final delivery.
• The "Revisions" step can be repeated until you are satisfied with the product at no additional cost, as long as the complexity and number of revisions is fair to both parties (no drastic changes such as redoing the product).
• Orders will be made in queue order and delivery priority.

Payments

Payments can be made through PayPal or Stripe either in advance or in halves:

• I will start working once at least the first half of the project price is paid.
• I will send you a preview each time i make substantial progress for feedback. If you paid full price up front, you can also request earlier versions of the product to try it out for yourself without waiting for final delivery.
• I will deliver the final product once the total price of the commission has been paid.
• During the 7 days following the final delivery you can request an extra revision for small adjustments at no additional cost.
